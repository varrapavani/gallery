Lyrebird

Mimicry Ability

It is one of the best natural mimics, capable of imitating chainsaws, camera shutters, alarms, and other birds' calls with incredible accuracy.

Appearance

The male has a long, lyre-shaped tail, which it uses during courtship displays.

Habitat

Found in Australian forests.

Special Feature

Its vocal learning ability is so advanced that it can fool predators and other birds.
