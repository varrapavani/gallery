Peregrine Falcon

Speed

is the fastest bird and the fastest animal on Earth, reaching speeds of over 380 km/h (236 mph) during its hunting dive (stoop).

Hunting Skill

It preys on other birds mid-air by striking them with a powerful impact.

Habitat

Found all over the world, including cities where it nests on tall buildings

Special Adaptation

It has a unique nose structure that prevents air from rushing in too fast at high speeds.
