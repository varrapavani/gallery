Arctic Tern

Migration

This bird has the longest migration of any animal, traveling from the Arctic to Antarctica and back every year (about 70,000 km or 43,500 miles).

Lifespan

Can live for over 30 years, meaning it can travel the equivalent of going to the Moon and back three times in its lifetime!

Diet

Feeds on fish and small marine creatures.

Special Adaptation

It has a light body and strong wings, allowing it to fly long distances without much energy use.
