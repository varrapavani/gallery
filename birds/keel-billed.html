Keel-billed Toucan

Beak Features

It has a large, colorful beak that looks heavy but is actually very light because it is made of keratin with hollow structures inside.

Diet

Mainly eats fruits but can also consume insects, eggs, and small reptiles

Habitat

Found in tropical rainforests of Central and South Ame

Special Ability

Uses its beak to grasp fruit from distant branches without needing to move much.
